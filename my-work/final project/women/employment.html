Starting a Job

Analyzes laws affecting women's decisions to enter the labor market.

This category saw the most reforms over the last ten years. Thirty five economies across every region introduced sexual harrassment laws protecting women at work. Of these five - Argentina, Bangladesh, Georgia, Malaysia and Moldova - introduced sexual harrassment laws, but did not provide for either criminal penalties or civil remedies for the violation of these laws. Additionally, nine economies introduced laws mandating nondiscrimination in employment based on gender.
