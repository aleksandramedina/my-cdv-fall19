Getting Paid

Measures laws affecting occupational segregation and the gender wage gap.

In this indicator, 13 economies - Albania, Belgium, Bolivia, the Comoros, Equatorial Guinea, Liberia, Libya, Mauritius, Montenegro, Serbia, South Africa, Vietnam and Zambia - introduced laws mandating equal remuneraton for work of equal value. Twenty two economies reformed to remove restrictions on women's wrok, reducing the likelihood that women are kept out of working in certain sectors of the economy. China removed all job restrictions on women. Five economies removed restrictions on women working in specific industries. Several of these reforms were motivated by the improved use of technology in these industries. However, Vietnam also introduced job restrictions on women working in agriculture, construction, energy, transportation and water.
